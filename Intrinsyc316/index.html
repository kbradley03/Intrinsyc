Welcome!

Intrinsyc is a multi-national company providing comprehensive solutions in the areas of startup and entrepreneur consulting, professional tech development, ecosystem design, youth tech programming and strategic organizational planning.

Intrinsyc utilizes strategic partnerships between the U.S. and the Southeast region of Europe to develop and facilitate technology ecosystems in underserved and/or under-utilized communities.
